How it works

Sign & settle

For business

Take crypto. Get paid in cash.

The Atomic Pay merchant gateway lets any business accept stablecoin payments in minutes — then withdraw them as real money, in your own currency, straight to your bank. Non-custodial the whole way: funds land in your wallet, never ours. Free, self-serve signup — no contracts to start.

🧾

Invoices & checkout

Email a customer a branded invoice with a hosted checkout link, or drop a pay button on your site. They pay in USDC, USDT or PYUSD from any wallet.

🏪

Point of sale

Turn any amount into a QR code and watch it flip to paid in real time. USDC on Base keeps fees to a few cents.

🏦

Cash out to your bank

Crypto in, cash out. Withdraw to your bank account or card in your local currency through licensed partners covering 160+ countries — you sell from your own wallet, so we never hold your money.

Filterable payment history, CSV export, and automatic customer receipts — plus signed webhooks so developers can wire payments into anything.
