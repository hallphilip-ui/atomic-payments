Privacy Policy

What we collect, why, and your choices.

Last updated: 7 July 2026 · Effective: 7 July 2026

The short version. Atomic is non-custodial and we try to collect as little as possible. We process wallet addresses and transaction details to run swaps and to screen for sanctions/fraud, plus basic analytics to improve the product. We don't sell your personal data. Note that blockchain transactions are public and permanent by nature — that's outside anyone's control.

1. Who We Are

Atomic ("we," "us," "our") operates the atomicpay.cloud interface for non-custodial crypto swaps. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Service.

2. Information We Process

CategoryExamplesWhy
Wallet & transaction dataPublic wallet address, source/destination assets and chains, amounts, quotes, transaction hashesTo generate quotes, route and execute swaps, show your history, and calculate fees
Compliance dataSanctions/risk-screening results for connected addresses, approximate location/regionTo meet legal obligations and prevent prohibited or illicit use

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, government ID, or seed phrase. We never have access to your private keys.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and secure the Service and process your swaps.
  • Screen for sanctions, fraud, and prohibited activity, and comply with law.
  • Understand and improve product usage (analytics and session replay).
  • Respond to support requests and communicate service changes.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use privacy-focused product analytics (currently PostHog) with cookies/local storage to understand usage, capture anonymized session replays, and diagnose errors. We also use Google Analytics for aggregate, anonymized site-usage measurement; it is configured to truncate IP addresses and to disable advertising and cross-device signals. Both are non-essential and gated on your cookie-consent choice: in the EU/EEA/UK/Switzerland nothing loads until you accept, and elsewhere you can decline or withdraw at any time from the cookie banner. You can also limit tracking through your browser settings or tracking-protection tools. We do not use advertising trackers. This notice covers both Atomic Pay (atomicpay.cloud) and Atomic Exchange (atomicexchange.cloud).

5. How Information Is Shared

  • Liquidity providers & blockchains. To execute a swap, transaction details are sent to the relevant Provider and broadcast on public blockchains, where they are permanently visible.
  • Service vendors. Analytics, hosting, email, and compliance/sanctions-screening vendors that process data on our behalf under contract.
  • Legal. When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud or illicit activity.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. On-Chain Data Is Public

Blockchain transactions — including wallet addresses, amounts, and hashes — are public, permanent, and outside our control. Anyone can view and analyze them. Please keep this in mind when transacting.

7. Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law (for example, compliance and financial-record obligations). On-chain data cannot be deleted by us or anyone else.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information we process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and you are responsible for securing your own wallet and keys.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or the CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object or lodge a complaint with a regulator. To make a request, contact us at support@atomicpay.cloud. Note we may be unable to identify you from a wallet address alone, and we cannot alter on-chain data.

10. International Transfers

We and our vendors may process information in countries other than yours. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes take effect when posted with a new "Last updated" date.

13. Contact

Privacy questions: support@atomicpay.cloud.
