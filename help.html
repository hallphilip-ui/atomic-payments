Frequently asked

The essentials — how Atomic works and what to do when something goes wrong.

How does a swap work?

Pick a from/to asset and amount, connect your wallet, and confirm. Your wallet signs the transaction — Atomic routes it across chains and settles to your destination address.

Is Atomic custodial?

No. Funds move directly from your wallet through the swap route. Atomic never holds your balance — you sign every transaction yourself.

What are the fees?

A flat platform fee of 2.5%. All-in cost is typically ~2.75% once the routing fee and network gas are included. The exact received amount is always shown before you confirm.

My wallet won't connect

Use a Chrome-based browser for now (a Safari connect issue is being fixed — see the tracker below). Make sure only one wallet extension is set as default, or pick your wallet from the connect list.

Which assets are supported?

15 assets are certified for live routing today — BTC, ETH, SOL, USDC, USDT, WBTC, DAI, UNI, LINK, ARB, OP, POL, BNB, AVAX. More are added as they're verified.
